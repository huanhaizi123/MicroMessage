Scan Wi-Fi
Q：What is Scan Wi-Fi？
A：Scan Wi-Fi is a convenient way to connect to the Internet by scanning Wi-Fi on WeChat and access Wi-Fi services provided by vendors and third-party network service providers..
Q：Is Scan Wi-Fi secure？
A：WeChat provides a convenient networking mode; however, be cautious when using public Wi-Fi and follow security instructions. Don’t:
  • 1）Open unknown links;
  • 2）Download unknown applications;
  • 3）Enter your password for WeChat, QQ or bank accounts on untrusted websites.
Q：How to enable Scan Wi-Fi？
A：

Select the prompted network on“Settings”-“Wi-Fi”on your device. After the network is enabled, you can return to WeChat and surf the Internet.

Q：How do I connect to the Internet via Scan Wi-Fi？
A：

Scan the vendor's Wi-Fi QR code on "DISCOVER"-"Scan QR Code".

Q：How do I quit Scan Wi-Fi？
A：Switch to other networks on "Settings"-"Wi-Fi" on your device or disable the Wi-Fi function.
